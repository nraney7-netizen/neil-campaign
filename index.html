Putting People Before Lobbyists — Why I’m Running for Mayor

Los Angeles deserves leadership that listens to its people, not big donors or lobbyists.

I grew up in neighborhoods where working families struggled to be heard. I’ve seen lobbyists and big donors make decisions behind closed doors — and I’m running to change that.

Too many Angelenos are forced to fight for basic needs while powerful insiders call the shots. I believe in leadership that is:

  • No corporate PAC money: Every decision answers to the people, not donors.
  • Community-driven: Solutions built with neighborhoods, for neighborhoods.
  • Transparent: City Hall that communicates openly and honestly.
“Neil listens to real people — not lobbyists.” — Maria R., LA Resident
