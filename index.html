About Neil Raney

Neil Raney has spent decades fighting for everyday Americans. From organizing local community initiatives to championing transparency in government, Neil has always put people first.

Running as a progressive outsider, Neil’s mission is clear: dismantle special interest influence, empower small businesses, and create opportunities for families across the nation.

His vision is a government that listens to the people — not lobbyists — and acts decisively to protect your future.
