About Neil

Neil Raney is running for President to restore government to the people. With a career rooted in community service and fighting for transparency, Neil believes leaders should be accountable to voters, not corporate lobbyists.

He is committed to protecting freedom, defending the Constitution, and ensuring every American has a voice in government. This campaign is about real reform, honesty, and empowering citizens.

My Priorities

  • End Corporate Control of Politics: Ban lobbyist money and restore fair elections.
  • Protect Working Americans: Raise wages, protect Social Security, and bring jobs home.
  • Defend Freedom and the Constitution: Protect free speech, privacy, and individual rights.
  • Affordable Healthcare: Ensure healthcare is accessible and affordable for all.
