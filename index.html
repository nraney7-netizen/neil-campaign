About Neil Raney

Neil is a progressive, anti-lobbyist candidate committed to putting people first. From housing to education, Neil leads with transparency and results.

Key Issues

Affordable Housing

Ensuring every Angeleno has a safe place to live.

Neil will expand affordable housing, regulate short-term rentals, and ensure equitable urban development.

Healthcare

Making healthcare accessible and affordable.

Neil supports expanding community clinics and mental health resources across Los Angeles.

Small Business Support

Championing local entrepreneurs and jobs.

Neil will reduce bureaucracy, provide micro-grants, and create a small-business advisory council.
