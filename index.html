Why I’m Running

I am running for President because our government no longer works for everyday Americans. Corporations and special interests have too much influence over our politics, while working families struggle to get ahead.

I believe the purpose of government is to serve the people, protect freedom, and ensure fairness. This campaign is about restoring honesty, transparency, and accountability to leadership.

My Priorities

  • End Corporate Control of Politics: Ban lobbyist money and restore fair elections.
  • Protect Working Americans: Raise wages, protect Social Security, and bring jobs home.
  • Defend Freedom and the Constitution: Protect free speech, privacy, and individual rights.
  • Affordable Healthcare: Ensure healthcare is accessible and affordable for all.
