Why I’m Running

Los Angeles should work for the people who live here. But too many decisions are being made behind closed doors by donors and insiders while regular families struggle.

I’m running for Mayor to change that — with no corporate PAC money, no lobbyists, and leadership that answers directly to the people.

  • No corporate PAC money

  • Transparent leadership

Our Priorities
