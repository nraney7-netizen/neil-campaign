Why Neil Is Running

Texas deserves leaders who put people first. Neil is running to bring honesty, clarity, and responsibility back to public office.

His mission is simple: ensure every Texan has a government they can trust — one that works in the open, listens to its citizens, and delivers real results.

Core Values

  • Honesty in every decision
  • Transparency in budgeting and policy
  • Accountability for public officials
  • Respect for individual rights
