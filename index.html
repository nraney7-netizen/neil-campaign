About Neil

Neil is running for President to return government to the people it serves. He believes America works best when leaders follow the Constitution, respect individual liberty, and tell the truth to voters.

This campaign is focused on restoring trust in government, defending free speech, and ending the culture of corruption and insider control in Washington.

Issues

  • Constitutional Government: Enforce the Constitution as written and stop executive overreach.
  • Economic Freedom: Reduce inflation, cut wasteful spending, and protect small businesses.
  • Free Speech: Defend the First Amendment and oppose censorship.
  • Anti-Corruption: End insider politics and revolving-door lobbying.
  • Secure Borders: Enforce the law with fairness and humanity.
