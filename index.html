About Neil

Neil Raney is running for President to restore government to the people. He has dedicated his career to community service and fighting corporate influence in politics.

Neil’s vision: a government accountable to citizens, defending freedom, protecting working families, and ensuring every American has a voice.

My Priorities

  • End Corporate Control: Ban lobbyist money and restore fair elections.
  • Protect Working Americans: Raise wages, protect Social Security, and bring jobs home.
  • Defend Freedom: Protect free speech, privacy, and individual rights.
  • Affordable Healthcare: Ensure healthcare is accessible for all.
